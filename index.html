About Us

Serving the Nation with Integrity

The Ministry of Interior is the principal government institution responsible for internal security, public order, border management, refugee affairs, and community safety across the Republic of South Sudan.

Under the leadership of Hon. Gen. Aleu Ayieny Aleu, appointed in January 2026, the Ministry is committed to restructuring and modernizing our security forces in preparation for the December 2026 general elections.

Our mandate is to uphold the rule of law, protect citizens' rights, and strengthen national cohesion through accountable, transparent, and service-oriented governance.
