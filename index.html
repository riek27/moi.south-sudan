About Us

Serving the Nation with Integrity

The Ministry of Interior is the principal government institution responsible for internal security, public order, border management, refugee affairs, and community safety across the Republic of South Sudan.

Under the leadership of Hon. Gen. Aleu Ayieny Aleu, the Ministry is committed to restructuring and modernizing our security forces in preparation for the December 2026 general elections.

Leadership

Ministry Leadership

Dedicated departments committed to national security and public service excellence.

🏛️

Minister of Interior

The political head of the Ministry, responsible for overall policy direction, strategic leadership, and representation of the Ministry in Cabinet and Parliament.

📋

Deputy Minister

Assists the Minister in executive duties, oversees special projects, and coordinates inter‑directorate activities to ensure seamless implementation of policies.

💬

Advisors

Provide expert counsel on political, security, and administrative matters to the Minister and senior leadership, ensuring informed decision‑making.

🔧

Technical Advisor

Offers specialised technical guidance on law enforcement modernisation, infrastructure, and capacity‑building initiatives within the Ministry.

💰

DG Administration & Finance

Manages human resources, budgeting, procurement, and financial accountability across all Ministry directorates and support services.

📊

DG Research, Planning & Statistics

Drives evidence‑based policy through data collection, strategic planning, and monitoring and evaluation of Ministry programmes.

⚖️

DG Legal Administration

Ensures legal compliance, drafts legislation and regulations, and provides legal advice to all directorates and the Minister's office.

📢

DG Public Relations

Manages communication with the public and media, promotes transparency, and handles crisis communication and public awareness campaigns.
